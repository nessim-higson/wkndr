WKNDR

Background Engines

Generative ambient-background R&D for the weather field — exploring what a distinctive, abstract, weather-by-inference background could be. Four directions, each a tunable playground.

Tap a card to open the full playground (control panel top-left). Built as self-contained canvas / WebGL. Animation runs live; some are static-by-default with a Motion toggle.

Weather Risograph

An abstract shape-language for weather — grain + colour-field geometry. Six conditions (sun/rain/cloud/wind/storm/snow), each its own motif; time-of-day, motion, and per-mode variants.

Gradient Forms
