I. Today + Mon only

A medieval day at the Muiderslot↗

Muiden · 25 min by train · 10:30 → 16:30

The castle goes fully medieval for two days only — sword-fighting school for kids, fire shows, troubadours, knightings in the courtyard. Your four-year-old can get knighted at the Bataille Knight School. Grounds are big enough to wander all day, with water on three sides and shade for the heat.

II. Season opens

The Fluistervink silent boat↗

Amsterdamse Bos · 25 min by bike · hourly

Electric whisper-boat through forest waterways — no engine, just water and birds, the occasional deer at the shore. One of the most peaceful hours the city offers. Just opened for the season this week. A four-year-old will sit still for it.

just opened for season, the forest stays cool while city warms, holds a kid's attention

III. Classic Sunday

Vondelpark + pancakes on the Amstel↗

Vondelpark + Pannenkoekenboot · 15 min by bike

The classic: bike Vondelpark, release the child onto the Groot Melkhuis playground, then ferry to the Pannenkoekenboot for lunch on the water. The boat does a 75-min loop down the Amstel while you eat all-you-can-eat pancakes. IJscuypje on the way home.

park weather, the boat contains the child, pancakes are the bribe
