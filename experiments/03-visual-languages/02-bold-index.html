Medieval days at the Muiderslot↗

Muiderslot, Muiden · outdoor castle grounds · €18 / €12 children

The castle goes full medieval for two days only — sword-fighting school for kids, fire shows, troubadours, battles in the courtyard. Your four-year-old can get knighted at the Bataille Knight School and make their own knight creation in the workshop.

The grounds are big enough to wander all day. Real moat, real towers, dragons not included.

The Fluistervink silent boat↗

Amsterdamse Bos · electric boat through forest waterways · €14 / €9

An electric whisper-boat through the forest — no engine noise, just water, birds, the occasional deer at the shore. One of the most peaceful hours you can buy in Amsterdam, and a four-year-old will sit still for it because there's so much to watch.

Just opened for the season. Bring snacks. After: rowing lake for ice cream or kayak at Bosbaan.

Why now Just opened for the season · forest stays cool when city gets hot · kid-magic factor
Book ahead · €14 / €9

Vondelpark + pancakes on the Amstel↗

Vondelpark + Pannenkoekenboot · bike-able loop · ~€30 family lunch

The classic Amsterdam Sunday: bike Vondelpark, let the four-year-old loose at the Groot Melkhuis playground, then ferry over to the Pannenkoekenboot for lunch on the water. The boat does a 75-min Amstel loop while you eat all-you-can-eat pancakes.

Kids get windows of boats and bridges; the boat itself is the adventure. IJscuypje on the way home.

Why now Sunny park weather · boat keeps kid contained · pancakes are the bribe
