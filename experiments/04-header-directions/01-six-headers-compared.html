How to read these six

Each comp keeps v5's top bar, filter strip, and the picks below identical — only the header zone changes. I and III are visual moves (atmospheric and generative). II and VI are temporal moves (clock-anchored and moment-aware, with VI adding editorial urgency). IV is a process move (the page showing its work). V is a functional move (the header becomes an input). I-IV-V get more interactive in different ways; II and VI are quieter but lean hard on a single ownable idea. The right answer depends on whether you want the header to be a painting, a clock, a cover, a process, an instrument, or a voice.
