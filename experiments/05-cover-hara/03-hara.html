Pick I · Today + Monday only · 25 min by train

Medieval days at the Muiderslot.

Muiderslot · Muiden·10:30 → 16:30·€18 / €12 kids

The castle returns to the thirteenth century for two days. Sword-fighting school for children, fire shows in the courtyard, troubadours along the keep. The Bataille Knight School will knight a four-year-old and send them home with a cardboard sword and a story. Pick II · Season just opened · 25 min by bike

The Fluistervink, through the forest.

Amsterdamse Bos · electric whisper-boat·hourly·€14 / €9 kids

An electric boat that makes no sound, drifting through forest waterways. The first sailings of the season opened this week. There is water, there are birds, there are deer at the shoreline if the morning is quiet. A four-year-old will sit still for this hour because there is too much to see. After, the rowing lake at Bosbaan is five minutes by foot, with ice cream and grass.

Why nowFirst weekend of the season · forest stays cool while the city warms.
Pick III · The Sunday default · 15 min by bike

Vondelpark, then pancakes on the Amstel.

Vondelpark + Pannenkoekenboot·book the boat 13:00·~€30 family

Bike the park, release the child onto the Groot Melkhuis playground, then ferry across to the Pannenkoekenboot for lunch on the water. The boat does a seventy-five-minute loop down the Amstel while you eat all-you-can-eat pancakes. The child gets windows full of boats and bridges. The boat is itself the adventure. IJscuypje on the way home if the kid has earned it. The kid will have earned it.
